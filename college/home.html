Campus Life

The mesmerizing all-embracing campus is of 650 acres, encompassed by boundary wall and ring road. The infrastructure of campus consists of administrative and academic buildings, workshops, community centre and residential area for accommodation of students and staff. Besides general infrastructure it includes elementary amenities such as post office, shopping complex, a school for children, dispensary, a campus temple, an auditorium with capacity of seating thousand people and sports complex with vast stretches of open area The students get the bes t of opportunities in the form of highly advanced courses, eminent faculty members, well-equipped laboratories, library, hostels and immense facilities to excel in research and development. Apart from academics, there are plethora of societies such as ED cell, Centre of excellence (GIS and remote sensing), ISTE (Indian Society for Technical Education), and Vision providing an elbow room for amplifying each student’s budding ideas. Highly scientific and innovative technology is used for teaching and carrying out research activities. Every year MANIT is enriched by the laurels brought by the faculty members and the students in the form of research publications, projects, fellowships and industrial exposure.
