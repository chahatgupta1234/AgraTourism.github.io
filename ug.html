UG Programme

MANIT Bhopal runs Courses of 04 years duration leading to the Bachelor's of Technology Degree in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Communication, Computer Science, Chemical, Material & Metallurgical Engineering and Bachelor's of Planning. The Duration of Bachelor's of Architecture is 05 years. MANIT Bhopal is also running a Dual degree 05 years program in Mathematics and Data Science Since 2021 onwards. The Syllabus and scheme of examination for these courses are as prescribed by the Senate.
